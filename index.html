Budget Formatting
Rosa Lenihan
Founder / Owner · Quantum Academy & Quantum College

Born into a family of educators in Taiwan and a scholarship recipient at SUNY Buffalo, Rosa founded Quantum Academy in 2009 after building a reputation for high standards in Richmond and Vancouver. Her philosophy — that rigorous academics and community responsibility are inseparable — shaped an institution focused on university preparation alongside deep civic engagement.

Tax & Finance
Jerry Hu
CPA · Co-founder, Everplus CPA Ltd.

With nearly a decade of public practice experience including time at Ernst & Young, Jerry advises individuals and small businesses on tax compliance, financial reporting, and estate planning. A 2023 graduate of the CPA In-Depth Tax Program, he specializes in Canadian corporate and personal tax planning, regularly supporting corporate reorganizations and succession planning for entrepreneurs and high-net-worth individuals.

Buying a House
Paisley Agnesini
REALTOR® · RE/MAX City Realty

Raised in New Westminster within a real estate family, Paisley developed hands-on mastery of property management long before earning her licence from the UBC Sauder School of Business. By 21 she had purchased her first home and successfully house hacked to build early equity. With a background in high-stakes contract negotiation from Vancouver's film industry, she now helps clients build long-term wealth through smart and transparent real estate decisions.
